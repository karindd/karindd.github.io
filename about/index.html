I love to solve problems, especially with design.

I am a designer with a passion for building usable and visually engaging digital products.

I have experience in the fields of marketing, graphic design, and UX design and research. Being a marketer-designer hybrid has allowed me to have both business needs and human-centered solutions at heart.

My dream is to make people's life easier with the help of technology. User research, design thinking and responsible design motivates me.

When I'm not researching, designing or writing front-end code, I enjoy developing new skills and catching up on the latest tech.

In my spare time you’ll find me volunteering for The Paw Portal (animal rescue organization in Barcelona), or illustrating with my cat by my side.
